# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
The writing tone throughout the portfolio should be **Casual and Conversational**. The copy should be approachable, engaging, and personal, allowing your unique personality to shine through.

## Branding Colors
The primary aesthetic will revolve around **Dark Mode**. The color palette should utilize deep backgrounds to make content pop, creating a sleek and modern visual experience.

## UX Principles
The core user experience will be driven by:
1. **Speed:** The application must be highly performant, ensuring fast load times and smooth transitions.
2. **Mobile Design:** A mobile-first approach is essential to provide a seamless experience on all device sizes.
3. **Interactivity:** Engaging micro-animations and interactive elements should be incorporated to create a dynamic and memorable feel.
